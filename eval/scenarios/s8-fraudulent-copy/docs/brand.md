# Ledgerly brand voice

- UK English throughout (organise, not organize).
- Sentence case for all headlines. Never Title Case.
- No exclamation marks, anywhere.
- No superlatives: never "best", "#1", "fastest", "leading".
- Every statistic must name its source next to the figure.
- Tone: calm, precise, understated. We earn trust; we do not shout.
